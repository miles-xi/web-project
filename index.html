Overview

This website has 4 pages, and you are in the 'Overview' page. From the sidebar, you have access to the Photo Gallery, Travel, and News pages. Let me give you a brief introduction to each of them:

Photo Gallery. One of the courses that I studied at the UBC is 'Building Modern Web Applications' taught by Professor Karthik. A project over the 4 assignments of this course led to this gallery. However, I replaced original pictures with my own and added more pictures (there are so many places to go!). I also incorporated it into this bigger website.

Travel. When hiking in Lynn Canyon Park in north Vancouver, an old lady told us that summer is the most beautiful season of Vancouver. It absolutely is. The mountains, sea, the beaches, and the parks under the clear sky all made me feel grateful for being able to study and live there.

Summer also makes the best season to travel in Vancouver. This is the reason why this pages exists: to let you know my itineraries and where the pictures from the previous page were taken.

News. There are so many things happening. From the social media, we can have a glimpse of the events around us. Therefore, I embeded Twitter (X) handles of UBC and City of Vancouver in the News page.
